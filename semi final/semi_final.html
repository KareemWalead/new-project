How we started and where we’re headed

Founded in 2012, Uppsala started with a simple goal: to help businesses grow
through innovative and effective marketing strategies. Over the years, we’ve
expanded from a small team of digital marketing enthusiasts to a full-service
agency with a global client base.

What started as a passion for digital marketing has grown into a mission to
drive success for brands across various industries. From startups to
established enterprises, we’ve helped our clients increase traffic, improve
conversions, and scale their businesses.
